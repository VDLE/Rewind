About Rewind

Rewind was created as part for Chase Carthen in completion of a Master's in Computer Science with the aid of Vinh Le. Rewind is a Automatic Music Transcription (AMT) system that converts your favorite audio files into a midi. Midi is commonly used by others as a highly portable format that can be shared amongst others easily. It is possible to get transcirptions or the notes of your favorite music. These notes can be turned into guitar tabs, sheet music, or simply replayed back by your favorite. This website has an in-built sythnesizer that you can use to play your transcriptions made by the system.


How Rewind Does Magic

Rewind is a tool that automatically converts a digital audio file (mp3,wav,au, and other formats) into a symbolic format such as a midi file. Rewind automatically turns your audio files into a spectrogram and then converts it too a piano roll.
